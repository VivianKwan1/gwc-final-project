WACK

That's the branding of Big Jam. We strive to tiptoe the line between being plain out weird and being hipster millenials. Photos of jam in odd settings, Warhol-esque clowns, and nostalgic childhood show characters are what sparked the overall theme of Big Jam.

For us, music is an outlet. It allows us to be inspired by others and to share our own creativity. Music has created a community and united people around the world through lyrics and rhythm. There are no boundaries to music; it can have a strong message, a weird message, or no message at all. We made Big Jam to let people explore a new playlist, whatever it may be.

JAM ON !
